1. Download 7zip
	2. Right click on Balatro.exe (in Steam > steamapps > common > Balatro) and select (On Windows 11, you 	   will have to select the "Show More Options" on the bottom of the drop down) 7Zip > Open Archive
	3. Drag and drop the folders (inside the "for repaint" folder) on to the first screen of the window
	   to replace
	4. Select yes to any confirmation boxes that may appear confirming
	   to replace files

After that, it should all be inserted and when you launch the game the edits should appear straight away.
Keep in mind that by doing this, you will have to revert your game to remove the mod.

You can do this by:
	1. Right-click on the game in your Steam library
	2. Select Properties...
	3. Navigate to the Installed Files tab
	4. Select Verify integrity of game files
